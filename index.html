Click the field once to give it keyboard focus, then press 1 or 2 on the mode menu to start.
1 Player (vs AI): WASD or Arrows to move, J = low kick, K = high kick.
2 Player: Player 1 — WASD to move, C = low kick, V = high kick. Player 2 — Arrow keys to move, O = low kick, P = high kick.
First to lead when the 60-second clock runs out wins. If it's level, you get 30 seconds of extra time; still level after that, and it comes down to a penalty shootout — a face-on view where the shooter moves the reticle to a zone (left/right to pick a side, up/down for low or a harder-to-stop high corner) and kicks, while the keeper moves the same way to dive toward a side and reach high or stay low. Press R to play again, or M for the mode menu, after full time. A ⭐ power-up star spawns every 10 seconds — kick the ball into it to grab a random buff: a taller goal, a speed boost, a giant player, or a freeze on your opponent. Camping in front of your own goal for too long is risky too — a ⚠ appears after 5 seconds, and staying past 7 gets you frozen as a penalty.
